POETRY API

How to Use:

Firstly, select what you want to search by with the buttons at the top.

"By Author" lets you find poems by the authors name, "By Contents" lets you find them by the lines inside the poem, and "By Titles" lets you find them by the titles. "Random" is a random poem generator.

Once you selected the author, contents, or titles button, please type in a name/line/title into the search bar, and click enter/the search button.
